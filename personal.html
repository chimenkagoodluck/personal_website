Who I am

About Me

I'm Chimenka Goodluck, a software engineer and researcher with a deep passion for building secure, intelligent systems. I work at the intersection of software engineering, machine learning, and cybersecurity — fields that I believe are converging to define the future of technology.

My research focuses on adversarial robustness in machine learning, privacy-preserving computation, and AI-driven security tooling. I enjoy translating academic ideas into real-world tools that developers and security teams can actually use.

When I'm not writing code or reading papers, I'm contributing to open source and mentoring early-career engineers across Lagos and beyond.
